Kalakriti- the Official Creative Arts Society of Delhi Technological University (Formerly DCE), is a society that brings together various art forms from different cultures and pour them on a single canvas, with a sole aim- "to spark creativity amongst everyone."

TEAM-CORE ARTS


The Core Arts team is full of extremely talented ART MENTORS excelling in different art backgrounds. All of the mentors are well established in their respective genres. The team's job to create and explore different artistic skills.

TEAM-CORPORATE


Team Corpo focuses mainly on making long term relationships through sponsorships and other possible monetary benefits for the seamless working of the Society. It exposes you to an environment that is interactive as well as experiential.

TEAM-DESIGN


The design team contributes to making templates for our Instagram posts and designing posters and videos for all the upcoming events. One can enhance his/her skills of designing and take them to a whole different level while being associated with the design team.

TEAM-PUBLIC RELATIONS


When it comes to representing the society, one can say that PR Team is the face of our Society. The goal of PR team is to build mutually beneficial relationships with Art societies of different Colleges and Universities across the country.
